COTTAGE CHEESECAKE

INGREDIENTS:

BOTTOM

  • 150 grams of biscuits
  • 80 grams of unsalted butter

CHEESECAKE

  • 6 sheets of gelatine
  • 500 grams of cottage cheese
  • 250 ml whipped cream
  • 2 tl vanilla paste
  • 4 el crystal sugar
  • 100 ml milk

TOPPING

  • 200 gram raspberries

INSTRUCTIONS

    FOR THE BOTTOM

  • 1. Crush the biscuits into the kitchen machine. Divide the crumb mixture over the bottom and press hard with the rouned part of a spoon. Put this bottom into the fridge.

  • 2. Melt the butter in a steelpan and add the crumbs to this. Stir well and pour this mixture into a grease and in baking paper covered spring form. Divide the crumb mixture over the bottom and press hard with the rouned part of a spoon. Put this bottom into the fridge.

  • 3. Divide the crumb mixture over the bottom and press hard with the rouned part of a spoon. Put this bottom into the fridge.

    • FOR THE FILLING

  • 1. Put the gelatine leaves into a bowl with cold water en soak it for around 10 minutes. Put the cottage cheese into a bowl. Beat in a second bowl the whipped cream with the vanilla paste and sugar to a stiff mixture.

  • 2. Heat the milk up slowly. Squeeze the gelatine leave and let this dissolve in the milk while stirring. Take the pan of the gas. Let the mixture cool a little bit. After this you can add the mixture while stirring to the cottage cheese.

  • 3. Beat the cream cheese creamy. In another bowl, beat the whipped cream to a firm consistency.

    • FINAL TOUCHES

  • 1. Stir the whipped cream through the cottage cheese. Crush the raspberries and add these as well. Pour the cottage cheese mixture over the bottom and put some extra raspberries on the top.

  • 2. Let the cake firm up in the fridge for 3 hours.
