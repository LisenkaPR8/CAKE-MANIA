LEMON CAKE

INGREDIENTS:

BISCUIT

  • 175 grams of egg
  • 100 grams of sugar
  • 6 grams of vanilla sugar
  • 25 grams of ma√Øzena

FILLING

  • 250 ml whipped cream
  • 3 tablespoons mascarpone
  • 3 tablespoons crystal sugar
  • 4-6 tablespoons egg liquor

DECORATION

  • powdered sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

    FOR THE BOTTOM

  • 1. Mix the eggs, sugar and vanilla sugar foamy and airy for around 10 minutes on the highest stand. While doing this, cover the baking form with baking paper

  • 2. Sift the flour and maizena and add this to the mixture. Stir this easy and careful through the mixture. Our the mixture into the baking form and smoothen out the top

  • 3. Bake the biscuit in around 25 to 30 minutes on 180 degrees. The biscuit is done when you can press the surface and it bounces back a little

  • 4. Leave the biscuit to cool for a couple of minutes in the baking form. After this, put the biscuit on a grate and let it cool completely

    • FOR THE FILLING

  • 1. Make the filling by putting the whipped cream, mascarpone and sugar in a bowl and beat it unitl the mixture has become stiff. Put this in the fridge until you need it

  • 2. Divide the mixture into horizontal halves, once it is cooled you can put the bottom on a plate

  • 3. Divide the whipped cream over the biscuit, followed by a layer of egg liquor.

    • FINAL TOUCHES

  • 1. Grab the top part of the biscuit. Carefully cut a star into the top by cutting it crossectional several times. Put this layer of biscuit on top of the cake

  • 2. Out the cake in the fridge until serving. Before serving, sprinkle the cake with powdered sugar to get a snowstar effect
