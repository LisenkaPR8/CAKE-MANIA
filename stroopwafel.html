STROOPWAFEL CHEESECAKE

INGREDIENTS:

BOTTOM

  • 150 grams of stroopwafel cookies
  • 80 grams of unsalted melted butter

CHEESECAKE

  • 300 grams of white chocolate
  • 400 grams of cream cheese
  • 160 grams of whipped cream
  • 150 grams of broken stroopwafel cookies

TOPPING

  • Caramel dessert sauce
  • Mini Stroopwafels

INSTRUCTIONS

    FOR THE BOTTOM

  • 1. Grease the spring form with butter and cover the bottom and sides of the spring form in baking paper.

  • 2. Crush the stroopwafel cookies in the kitchen machine and mix this with the melted butter.

  • 3. Divide this consistency over the bottom of the spring form. Press it with the rounded side of a spoon and put it in the fridge.

    • FOR THE FILLING

  • 1. While the bottom is cooling, melt the white chocolate au bain marie.

  • 2. Beat the cream cheese creamy. In another bowl, beat the whipped cream to a firm consistency.

  • 3. Add the melted chocolate and mix until the chocolate is completely adapted into the mixture.

  • 4. Stir the broken pieces of stroopwafel through the mixture and pour it over the cold cookie bottom.

  • 5. Smooth the top and put the cake in the fridge for at least 8 hours.

  • 6. Divide the caramel sauce over the cake in stringy lines.

    • DECORATION

  • 1. Divide the mini stroopwafels into halves and decorate the top of the cake.

  • 2. Remove the spring form and baking paper and serve the cake.
