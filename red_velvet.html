RED VELVET

INGREDIENTS:

BATTER

  • 120 grams unsalted butter
  • 250 grams crystal sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 50 grams of cacao powder
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • red food coloring
  • 250 grams of buttermilk
  • 300 grams flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of lemon juice

TOPPING

  • 400 grams cream cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

    FOR THE CAKES

  • 1. Beat the butter and sugar creamy, then add the eggs one by one and mix these until they are dissolved.

  • 2. Make a little paste of the cacao powder, vanilla extract, food colouring an perhaps a little bit of the buttermilk. Stir until a smooth mixture is created. Add this to the butter mixture and mix this until it is fully dissolved.

  • 3. In a bowl, you add the flour, baking soda and salt together, stir for a little bit. Put the buttermilk and lemon juice together in a measuring cup. Add the flour and milk slowly while you keep on mixing it together.

  • 4. Take three baking forms, grease these with a little bit of butter and put a baking paper on the bottom. Divide the batter evenly over all of the forms.

  • 5. Bake the cakes in around 30 minutes on 170 degrees in the oven.

  • 6. Let the cakes cool for about 10 minutes in the forms, then put them on a grid in order for them to cool down completely.

    • FINISHING TOUCHES

  • 1. First cut the rounded edges of the cakes, put these in a bowl and put them aside, you will need these later. Divide the cakes in horizontal halves zo that you have 5 pieces of cake.

  • 2. Put the first piece of cake on a plate and divide 3 table spoons of the frosting over this. Thin layers will be enough. Put another piece of cake on top of this and fill this one in the same manner. Continue this way until all of the pieces of cake are done.

  • 3. The rest of the frosting will be used for the top and sides of the cake. As decoration we will use the cake rests that we have put aside. Grab the pieces of cake en make crumbs of this. Once you have enough crumbs, you can decorate the cake as you like.
