ABOUT CAKE-MANIA

I realized that there is one thing that can always lift my spirits during these difficult quarantine times Baking cakes! Not only because you can make the most delicious creations, but mainly because you can have so much fun doing this together with the people that have to stay at home with you. As the decision to bake something always comes spontaneously while I am already in the supermarket, I wanted to give the people of CAKE-MANIA many more delicious options than just the standard packages of Dr. Oetker or Albert Heijn itself. With this app you can now quick and easy find the most amazing cake recipes that do not require hours of your time or loads of money. Beginning and enthusiastic bakers can become skilled experts within no time!
