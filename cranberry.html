Cranberry Cake

INGREDIENTS:

BATTER

  • 200 grams of unsalted butter
  • 200 grams of light brown caster sugar
  • 300 grams of self rising flour
  • 1 tablespoon of gingerbread spices
  • little pinch of salt
  • 1/2 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

FILLING

  • 1,5 kilo of sweet apples
  • 75 grams of crystal cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 100 grams of dried cranberries

INSTRUCTIONS

    FOR THE BOTTOM

  • 1. Put all of the ingredients for the dough in a bowl and knead until a firm dough.

  • 2. Grease the spring form and dust this with the flour.

  • 3. Divide the dough into 3 parts. Roll the first part and cover the bottom with this.

  • 4. With the second piece of dough, you cover the sides.

  • 5. Peel the apples, remove the cores and cut them into slices. Put all of the slices apple into a big bowl and mix with the sugar, cinnamon, cranberry compote and dried cranberries.

  • 6. Divide the bread crumbs over the bottom of the cate, followed by the apple filling.

  • 7. Put little holes between the slices of apple to prevent sagging in the oven. Press the filling firmly

    • FINAL TOUCHES

  • 1. Roll the last piece of dough en cut this into strips. Lay these strips in crosses on top of the cake.

  • 2. Bake the pie in 60 minutes on 170 degrees in the oven.

  • 3. Let the cake cool down in order for it to get even more firm.
